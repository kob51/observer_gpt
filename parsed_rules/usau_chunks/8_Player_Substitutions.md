## 8. Player Substitutions

<!-- section: 8 source: usau -->

### 8.A. Player substitutions can be completed only:
<!-- rule: 8.A source: usau -->

    - **8.A.1.** after a goal and before the substituting team has signaled readiness; or
      <!-- rule: 8.A.1 source: usau -->
    - **8.A.2.** to replace injured players, replace players with illegal equipment (7.D.3), to resolve
      <!-- rule: 8.A.2 source: usau -->
an incorrect personnel violation (9.C.1), or to replace a player removed due to
misconduct (2.C.2, Appendix C). In this case, the opposing team may substitute a like
number of, or fewer, players.
### 8.B. Substitutions are not permitted following a re-pull call, unless in accordance with 8.A.2.
<!-- rule: 8.B source: usau -->
